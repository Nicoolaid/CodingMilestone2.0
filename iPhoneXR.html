iPhone XR

The iPhone XR, launched in 2018, featured a 6.1-inch Liquid Retina display and came in a variety of vibrant colors. It offered advanced technology like Face ID, the powerful A12 Bionic chip, and an impressive camera system, all at a more affordable price point compared to other iPhone models of its time. The XR's camera system included a 12MP rear camera with Smart HDR and Portrait Mode capabilities, delivering high-quality photos and videos. Additionally, it had excellent battery life, wireless charging, and water and dust resistance (IP67 rating). The iPhone XR combined performance, style, and affordability, making it a popular choice among consumers looking for a high-quality smartphone experience without the heavy price tag.
